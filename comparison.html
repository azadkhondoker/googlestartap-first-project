How to choose the right Pixel for you.

Whether you’re looking for the best possible Pixel camera or a more affordable phone, Google Pixel offers options to suit your needs.

You use your phone for so many things, it makes sense to choose one designed to meet your own unique needs.

Google Pixel phones are built for day-to-day life, whether you’re taking photos of loved ones, setting goals for a new year, screening calls, or just looking to keep your , browsing activity safe . All Pixel phones have cameras with amazing image quality and built-in Google AI to make them even better. And the long-lasting battery means you can go all day without needing a charge. The Google Tensor chip in every phone delivers fast, responsive performance with helpful features that can save you time and make things easier. And because Pixel phones are protected by multiple layers of security and the Titan M2™ security chip, you also get peace of mind.

But not everyone has exactly the same needs. So Pixel comes in different models to best suit different daily needs and budgets. “With Pixel, our goal is to build the right product for the right user at the right price,” says Peter Prunuske, Google Senior Director in Product Management.

Pixel 7 Pro, Pixel 7, Pixel 7a, and Pixel Fold

Whether you want to take pro-level photos or you’re looking for value, you have options. Here’s how to find the Pixel that’s perfect for you.

“We’re harnessing Google’s state-of-the-art AI to understand your needs and make life easier.”

-Peter Prunuske, Google Senior Director in Product Management

Everything you need

If you’re keeping an eye on your budget but still want great performance, Pixel 7a is Google’s most affordable phone, delivering just the right amount of features for the price.

“It’s designed for users who want great performance at a great price, but who may not need the full capability of a flagship phone,” says Prunuske.

Pixel 7a is smaller than other Pixel phones, so you can hold it comfortably in one hand and easily unlock it with Face Unlock. It comes with 128 GB of internal storage. Most importantly, Pixel 7a has the Google Tensor chip , which makes apps launch fast, pages and images load quickly, and everything run smoothly. Pixel 7a lets you create vibrant videos, receive crystal clear calls from friends, or browse the web and use apps to find what you need – super fast. Live Translate can help you read signs, menus, and more in over 20 languages, while the powerful Adaptive Battery can last all day – and up to three days with Extreme Battery Saver – without having to plug in.

Everything you want

When you want a more powerful camera, more premium materials and finishes, and more storage options, pick a Pixel 7 . It’s fast and responsive, and brings pro-level capabilities to your streaming videos and favorite photos. And you can keep those memories on hand with storage options up to 256 GB.

“Pixel 7 delivers great value without compromising on what users want, with a premium design and quality and an optimized Google experience,” says Prunuske. Driving those experiences is Tensor G2, Google’s custom-built chip that allows phones to be faster and more intelligent.

Pixel 7 also conveys everyday luxury without costing too much, and the camera is more powerful than the Pixel 7a camera. Its dual rear-camera system includes a 50-megapixel wide lens and Super Res Zoom, which provides image quality similar to a dedicated 2x optical zoom,

Its improved front-facing camera gives you more options for selfies, and Cinematic Blur adds a dramatic effect to your photos and videos. With the Google Tensor G2 chip on board, Pixel 7 remedies shaky shots with Photo Unblur and removes unwanted items from your pictures with a few taps, thanks to Magic Eraser You can charge your Pixel 7 wirelessly.

For even more security in your browsing and other online activity, Pixel 7 and 7 Pro offer VPN by Google One , making them the most secure and private phones from Google.

The best-of-everything phone

Pixel 7 Pro is for those who want next-level photography, performance, display, and storage, plus exclusive features you won’t find in other Pixel phones. Pixel 7 Pro has a beautiful, sophisticated design and is crafted with a premium polished aluminum finish on the camera bar and housing.

“This is our ‘best-in-class,’” says Prunuske. “We’re taking advantage of the newest tech to elevate the overall experience.”

Pixel 7 Pro has a pro-level triple rear camera system that includes a 5x telephoto lens capable of images comparable to a 10x optical zoom So you can get high-quality pictures even if you’re far away – like sitting in the nosebleed section for a game or concert. Plus, an upgraded ultrawide lens with autofocus that powers Macro Focus – a new and exclusive feature on Pixel 7 Pro that lets you get vivid closeups of objects when you’re as near as three centimeters away.

It’s also a speedy phone, with 12 GB RAM and up to 512 GB of storage. That makes it easy to multitask and switch seamlessly between apps. And you’ll see all of it on the phone’s super-sharp 6.7-inch display and its higher brightness. With its variable refresh rate up to 120Hz (up to 90Hz on Pixel 7), you get responsive gaming, and smooth scrolling, without killing your battery. 

Productivity and multitasking on a whole new level

Pixel Fold, the only foldable phone engineered by Google, combines the best features of Pixel phones with an immersive inner display that helps you accomplish more in less time. Pixel Fold looks and acts like other Pixel phones when it’s closed. But when you open it up, you’ll find that it’s anything but ordinary. With features like Split Screen, drag and drop, and Taskbar, it takes multitasking to a whole new level. The amazing Pixel camera, now in a foldable, offers features like Rear Camera Selfie and hands-free modes like tabletop astrophotography to capture the stars.

Open up Pixel Fold to play videos on its large inner screen. And when it’s in tabletop mode, you can sit back and take in your favorite movies and shows without needing to hold the phone.

Pixel Fold is built to last, with Corning® Gorilla® Glass Victus™ on the outer screen and an IPX8 water resistance rating. And it’s the thinnest foldable, with a slim body that fits perfectly in your pocket or in your hand.

A phone that keeps getting better

Regardless of which Pixel you choose, you’ll get innovative software that draws on Google AI. So you can free up time with Call Screen , have better calls with Clear Calling, remove distractions from photos with Magic Eraser, navigate unfamiliar languages with Live Translate, and even get help fast with Car Crash Detection, which will call emergency services if it senses you’ve had an accident.

“We’re harnessing Google’s state-of-the-art AI,” says Prunuske, “to understand your needs and make life easier.”

And your phone keeps getting better and stays useful longer, with new and improved features every few months through Feature Drops. At every tier, Pixel owners get terrific value.

Takeaways

Pixel 7a is Google’s most affordable phone, delivering just the right amount of features for the price

The flagship phone – Pixel 7 Pro – pulls out all the stops to deliver the best of Google

Pixel Fold, the only foldable phone engineered by Google, folds power and innovation into one

Pixel 7

Built to perform. Priced just right.
